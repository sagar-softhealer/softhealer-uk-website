Odoo 18 has officially landed, bringing a wealth of features designed to make enterprise resource planning faster, smarter, and more integrated than ever before.

## 1. Redesigned User Interface
The first thing you will notice in Odoo 18 is the refined user interface. Navigation has been streamlined, views load significantly faster, and search filters are now much more intuitive.

## 2. Integrated AI Capabilities
Generative AI is now built directly into the core workflows:
* **Auto-drafting invoices:** Odoo can read scanned PDF invoices and automatically match them to purchase orders.
* **Email assistance:** Customer service reps can draft AI replies to incoming queries directly inside the discuss module.

## 3. Localization and Compliance
For UK businesses, compliance is key. Odoo 18 features updated VAT reporting structures and enhanced HMRC integration, making end-of-year accounts submission direct and painless.
